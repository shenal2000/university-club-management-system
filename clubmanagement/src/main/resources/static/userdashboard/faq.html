Frequently Asked Questions



The Club Management System is a digital platform that helps manage student clubs, memberships, events, announcements, and communication efficiently.

Students, club leaders, and administrators of the university can use the system based on their assigned roles.

After logging in, go to the Clubs section, select your preferred club, and click the “Join Club” button. Approval may be required by the club admin.

Yes, students are allowed to join multiple clubs unless restricted by university policies.

Upcoming events are displayed in the Events section on the dashboard after you log in.

Only authorized club leaders and administrators can create or manage events and announcements.

Click on the “Forgot Password” option on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
