About the Club Management System

The Club Management System is designed to make student club activities easier, more organized, and more accessible. It allows students to discover clubs, stay updated with events, and engage in university life through a single digital platform.

By connecting students, club leaders, and administrators, the system helps improve communication, reduce paperwork, and encourage active participation in clubs at NSBM Green University.
